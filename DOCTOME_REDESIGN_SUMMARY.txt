================================================================================
                          HOW TO CUSTOMIZE
================================================================================

1. REPLACE LOGO:
   See: CUSTOMIZE_LOGO_INSTRUCTIONS.md
   
2. CHANGE COLORS:
   Find "bg-blue-600" in page files, replace with desired color
   
3. ADJUST LOGO SIZE:
   Change h-16, h-24, or h-12 to:
   • h-12 = 48px
   • h-16 = 64px
   • h-20 = 80px
   • h-24 = 96px
   
4. CHANGE LANGUAGE:
   Edit French text in page files (JSX components)

Q: Where is the logo file?

Q: How do I replace the logo?
A: See CUSTOMIZE_LOGO_INSTRUCTIONS.md (3-step process)

Q: Can I change the blue color?
